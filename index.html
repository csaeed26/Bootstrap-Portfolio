About Me

My name is Craig Saeed, I am originally from Chicago, IL. My goal or reason for taking this course is to relaunch my career as a developer. Having worked as a front end developer for many years and not continuing to train and upgrade my skills, I killed my career as a web developer. I was laid off earlier this year and quickly realized I needed training if I ever planned to work as a web developer ever again.

Hopefully, by doing these things I am able to return to work and better support my family. Losing my job was a mental knockout and these last seven months have really brought me to a true humbling place in my life. I have learned that in order to be successful you must "Bullet-Proof" your career. The little things count, the networking, coder sites & blogs, the conventions etc, all matter. If I could speak to any aspiring developer my biggest advise would to "stay hungry, learn as much as you can for as long as you can".
